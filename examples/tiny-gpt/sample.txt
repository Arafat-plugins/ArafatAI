ArafatAI is a personal AI system.
The goal is to learn by building every layer by hand.
The browser agent can click, type, upload, inspect, and take screenshots.
The memory agent saves lessons from real debugging.
The eval agent checks whether an output is good enough.
The coding agent proposes patches, but the human reviews and merges.
Safe self-improvement means branch, patch, test, pull request, and human approval.
Tiny GPT teaches tokenizer, attention, loss, training, checkpoint, and generation.
ArafatAI grows first through tools, memory, evals, and disciplined engineering.
Large model training needs much more data, GPU compute, and evaluation.
